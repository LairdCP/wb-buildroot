Summit Data Communications provides embedded Wi-Fi solutions that are proven in business-critical mobile devices worldwide

Summit Data Communications is a provider of industrial-grade and medical-grade wireless modules. Summit’s rugged Wi-Fi products provide secure and reliable wireless connectivity for devices such as data collection terminals and medical equipment that operate in challenging environments such as factories, warehouses, and hospitals. More than one million Summit wireless modules are in use today at companies of all sizes around the world.
